Frequently Asked Questions

How do I ensure the vegetables stay fresh after delivery?

We recommend storing leafy greens in airtight containers or wrapping them in a damp cloth. Root vegetables like carrots and potatoes should be kept in a cool, dry place. For longer freshness, refrigerate perishables as soon as you receive them.

Do you offer organic vegetables?

Yes! We source our vegetables from trusted farms that use organic and pesticide-free practices. Each product page mentions whether the vegetable is organic.

What are your delivery areas and timings?

We deliver to major cities and surrounding areas. Deliveries usually take 24-48 hours depending on location. You can check delivery availability at checkout.

Can I customize my vegetable order?

Absolutely! You can handpick your vegetables or choose from our curated vegetable boxes. If you have special requests, let us know during checkout.
